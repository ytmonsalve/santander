Top Sights in Santander

Barichara

Barichara is one of the prettiest colonial towns in the region of Santander. It does not offer many tourist destinations but it has a very laidback atmosphere that makes tourist enjoy it. Here are some of the places you can enjoy while in Barichara.

Viewpoint

Enjoy the view of the area where Barichara is located on the brim of Suarez Canyon.

Cafe Albania

Tour Cafe Albania, a coffee plantation and farm in the outskirts of Barichara.

Camino Real

Hike El Camino Real from Barichara to Guane, easy walk between two colonial villages.

San Gil

San Gil is named the tourist capital of the region of Santander. It is known for having the most outdoor activities such as kayakying, hiking, and caving. Below are some of the many activities you can do while visiting San Gil.

Paragliding

Paraglide over the Chicamocha canyon, the second largest canyon in the world.

Rafting

Raft on white water river, Fonse River. Great for first timers, and nature lovers.

Juan Curi

Rappel down the Juan Curi waterfall, about 75 meters high. Enjoy the cold water in the heat of San Gil.

Bucaramanga

Bucaramanga is the largest city of the deparment of Santander. Colonial buildings surround downtown area like churches, houses, and more. Below are some popular tourist sites found in Bucaramanga.

Cathedral of the Holy Family

Visit the beautiful cathedral in the center of Bucaramanga. Stroll through Santander Park after your visit!

Bicentenario Bridge

Drive through the Bicentenario bridge at night to view it's spectacular lights. This bridge is visible from the whole city.

Santisimo

El Santisimo is one of the biggest attractions in Bucaramanga. Visit it's ecopark and ride on the cableway.

Zapatoca

Zapatoca is a common stop between Bucaramanga and San Gil. It's a calm town not known to a lot of travelers but worth the visit. Below are some of the places visited by locals and tourists.

Cueva del Nitro

Explore the cave of the Nitro, beautiful cave with lots of history and ancient markings.

Guanes Viewpoint

Enjoy the views of Zapatoca and its surrounding towns from the viewpoint peak. At night the city lights are spectacular.

Quijote Museum

Learn about Don Quijote's adventures through 45 sculptures featuring his adventure mates.
