Colombian Culture

The Colombian Spirit

Enjoyment for life

Colombians are usually seens as very positive people, often described as having an enjoyment for life. They normally brag at their ability to make a celebration out of any situation. Dancing is big part of their spirit, every time there is party or celebration the dance floor will be full.

Sports

Futbol

The most popular sports in the country are soccer (futbol), cycling, and motorsports. Colombians are very fond of soccer games and watch the games religiously. There is always big celebrations when the team scores and they wear their jerseys proud.

Music

Vallenato, Reggaeton, Cumbia

There are many musical genres in Colombia such as Vallenato, Salsa, Cumbia, Joropo, Cumbia, Rock, and more. The typical colombian genre is Vallenato specially in the Santander region. The main instrument used in Vallenato is the accordion. Colombia has many great artist such as Shakira, Karol G, Silvestre Dangond, J Balvin, Maluma, and more.
